A Path Out Of Poverty

About Our Project

Poverty is a complex issue that affects millions worldwide. This project highlights key causes, effects and practical ways to combat poverty through education , job creation and community develpment.

Our Vision

To see a world where every person has equal access to education , opportunities, and resources.

Our Objective

  • Educate communities on poverty reduction strategies
  • Promote vocational and financial literacy programs
  • Empower youth and women through skill development
